üçö How to Cook Pungar Rice

Follow these simple steps to prepare Pungar rice:

  1. Rinse the rice thoroughly to remove any impurities.
  2. Soak the rice in water for 4-6 hours to reduce cooking time and enhance its texture.
  3. Use a 1:3 rice-to-water ratio for cooking.
  4. Simmer on low heat for 30-40 minutes until fully cooked.
  5. Serve hot with vegetables, curries, or enjoy as a porridge.
